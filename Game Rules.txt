



	  GAME RULES:
	
	- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
	- The predefined winning score is 50
	  Players can set the winning score,
	  so that they can change the predefined score of 50.
	- In each turn, a player rolls two dices as many times as he whishes.
	  Each result get added to his ROUND score.
	- BUT, if the SUM of two dices is 6 or 8, all his ROUND score gets lost.
	  After that, it's the next player's turn.
	- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score
	  gets added to his GLBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
	- The first player to reach winning score on GLOBAL score wins the game.
	- A player looses his ENTIRE score when he rolls two 7 in a row.
	  After that, it's the next player's turn.
